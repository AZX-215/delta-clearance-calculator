Calculation notes

Clearance calculation used: 30 + sag − (span × 0.12), where sag is interpolated from the selected conductor/ruling-span table.

If calculated clearance is below 5 ft 0 in, the 5 ft 0 in minimum is applied. The suggested distance is then rounded up to the next whole inch.

If the actual span is outside the selected sag table range, the calculator extrapolates from the nearest two table points and flags it. Treat extrapolated results as a check item.
